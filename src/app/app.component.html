We the Good Shepherd Sisters have been in mission for nearly 160 years, helping Women and Childern, who experience the pressing needs of today including empowerment and skill development, poverty, human trafficking, forced migration, refugees, gender inequality, violence towards women and childern, and religious intolerance.

  • Through years of experience, we've initiated skill development programs aimed at equipping young women with the tools they need to thrive.Now, we are expanding our efforts through a small-scale business venture, where our students will take leadership roles, managing and developing this initiative.This empower them to become confident entrepreneurs, ready to engage with society

"Shepherd's Delight" was born from the Good Shepherd "Pastures", where young women and nutured with love and guided to develop their skills.Here, they are encouraged to be bold and enterprising as they embark on new ventures that generate income and sustainability. Your purchase from "Shepherd's Delight" directly supports our mission, contributing to the empowerment of countless young women, helping them lead independent and fulfilling lives.

Our Objectives

Good Shepherd Bakery is dedicated to offering a fresh culinary experience with our exceptional, handcrafted baked goods. Our mission is to elevate the local community's dining experience while creating a warm, vibrant atmosphere where everyone can indulge in the art of baking.
